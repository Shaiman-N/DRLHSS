**Simulated Threat Demo**:
```
"Wolf, show me a demo threat"
```

**Permission Request Appears**:
```
Wolf: "Alpha, I've detected a potential threat.

      Threat: Suspicious network scan
      Source: 203.0.113.45
      Severity: Medium
      Confidence: 87%

      This IP is performing automated port scanning, typical of
      reconnaissance activity before an attack.
      
      I recommend blocking this IP address immediately.

**Your Options**:
- **"Yes"** or **"Approve"**: Wolf blocks the IP
- **"No"** or **"Deny"**: Wolf respects your decision
- **"Tell me more"**: Get additional details

**Try It**:
Click "Approve" or say "Yes"

**Wolf's Response**:
```
"Understood, Alpha. IP 203.0.113.45 is now blocked.
 I'll continue monitoring for similar activity.
 You made the right call."

## Quick Tips

### 1. Permission System
- Wolf NEVER acts without your approval
- You can always deny recommendations
- Wolf learns from your decisions
- Emergency situations have 30-second timeout

### 2. Explanations
- Wolf adapts to your expertise level
- Ask "Why?" for more details
- Request technical or simple explanations
- All recommendations include evidence

### 3. Voice Interaction
- Wake word: "Hey Wolf"
- Speak naturally
- Wolf understands context
- Can interrupt with "Stop" or "Cancel"

### 4. Daily Briefings
- Automatic every morning at 8 AM
- Generate on-demand anytime
- Includes video presentation
- Shareable with team

### 5. Network Visualization
- 3D interactive view
- Color-coded by threat level
- Click devices for details
- Real-time updates
